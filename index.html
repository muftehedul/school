About Us:

Nageswari Doyamoyee Pilot Academy

Nageswari Doyamoyee Pilot Academy is an academic institute located at West nageswari (monir char) Nageshwari Kurigram. Its institute code (EIIN) is 122341. It was established on 01 January, 1946. Its co-education type is Combined. The institute has following 3 disciplines : Science, Business Studies, Humanities. Average exam passing rate of the institute is : JSC/Equivalent: 96.48%, SSC/Equivalent: 95.11%, Its MPO number is 8806041301. It has Day shift. Its management is Adhoc.

Mission:

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop young men with active and creative minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others, and the courage to act on their beliefs. We stress the total development of each child: spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional, and physical.

Vision:

Our Vision

Our vision is to develop well rounded, confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential. We will do this by providing a welcoming, happy, safe, and supportive learning environment in which everyone is equal and all achievements are celebrated.

The Computer Club is an educational and fun group. Our purpose is to learn more about computers. The club explores both computer architecture and new application software.
